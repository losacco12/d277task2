Geography


[Image of llinois location on the US map]. Ontheworldmap.com. (n.d.).https://ontheworldmap.com/usa/state/illinois/illinois-location-on-the-us-map.jpg

Illinois is located in the midwest. It is bordered by Lake Michigan to the northeast and the Missippi River to the west. It shares borders with Indiana, Wisconsin, Iowa, Kentucky, and Missouri. Illinois is mostly flat and, prior to urbanization, it consisted of mostly tallgrass plains. Illinois has many branches and subsidiary branches of the Mississippi River running through it which makes the soil rich for farming.

History

Illinois was the 21st state to join the union. It officially became a state in 1818. It has had 3 different cities serve as its capitol:

  1. Kaskaskia (1818-1820)
  2. Vandalia (1820-1839)
  3. Springfield (1839-present)

Population

The population of the state of Illinois is approximately 12,550,000 people. This population is heavily centered in the Chicago metropolitan area which has a population of around 9,440,000 people. The Chicago Metro area is in the northeastern corner of the state and includes small regions in both Wisconsin and Indiana. Outside of the Chicago metro area the majority of the state is rural and used for agriculture.

Income

The median household income in the state of Illinois of $78,433 is higher than the national median household income of $74,580.
